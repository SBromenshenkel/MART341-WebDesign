How To Make Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwhich!

    INGREDIENTS NEEDED!

    - Bread of your choice

    - Peanut Butter

    - Jelly of your choice

    DIRECTIONS!

    1. Lay two pieces of bread on a plate

    2. Cover one piece of bread in a even layer of your peanut butter

    3. Cover the plain piece of bread with your jelly

    4. Lay your bread on top of one another, with jelly and peanut butter sides facing each other

    OPTIONAL: CUT BREAD INTO TRIANGLES
